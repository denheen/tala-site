INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect limited information necessary to operate and improve the Service. Information falls into two categories: information you provide and information collected automatically.

A. Information You Provide

  • Email address or account identifiers (if you choose to create an account)

B. Information Collected Automatically

This information is collected through tools such as Firebase and Cloudflare to ensure app stability and performance.
